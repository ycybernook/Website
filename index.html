About Us

YANYAN'S
CYBER

We are Yanyan's Cybernook — a neighborhood hub in Brgy. Alingaro, Gen. Trias City, Cavite. More than just an internet café, we're a creative center for our community.

We offer internet access, layout design, printing, bills payment, and a growing line of personalized products available on Shopee and TikTok Shop — from keychains and mugs to stickers and photo cards.

🎯 Mission

To provide the community affordable access to Internet resources and quality personalized products and services.

🚀 Vision

To be the leading creative service hub in Gen. Trias — delivering excellent, up-to-date services in a homelike environment.
